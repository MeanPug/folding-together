How Can I Help

  • Download Folding@Home

    The primary way to contribute is by downloading folding@home and utilizing the CPU power in your computer to power simulations by researchers. These calculations are enormous and every little bit of power helps! Every simulation is like a lottery ticket, so the more tickets we buy the better our chances of jackpot.

    Download Learn More

  • Donate

    We'll contribute computing power to folding@home on your behalf. By donating, every dollar you provide will go towards purchasing resources on the cloud to run folding@home simulations. Upon donating, we'll provide you with a donor report that shows exactly how much work your dollars enabled.
