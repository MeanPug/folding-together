Folding@Together Makes It Easy To Power Research

The folding@home project and its contemporaries (like the rosetta@home project) allow anyone to donate their unused computing resources to researchers searching for new drug therapies. By downloading and running a simple piece of software, you can turn your computer into a powerful tool for researchers.

However, for some it can be a daunting and scary task to lend computing power in this way. While the software itself is totally safe and the simplest, cheapest way to contribute, folding@together is an option for those who want to help but are still hesitant about downloading and running the required software.

What we do

By leveraging the power of the cloud, we allow donors to provide researchers with valuable computing power without running any of the folding software themselves. Through microdonations from $1 - $100, we purchase and run computing resources on donors' behalf. In the interest of total transparency, we then provide all donors with a report detailing exactly how much work their donation enabled.
